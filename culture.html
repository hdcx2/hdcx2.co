Native Hawaiian Culture

---------

Language: The Hawaiian language (ʻŌlelo Hawaiʻi) is central to the culture, with efforts ongoing to revive and preserve it. Many place names and cultural practices are expressed in Hawaiian.

Hula: This traditional dance form tells stories through movement and chants (mele).
There are two main types:  
         ✧ Hula Kahiko (ancient hula)
         ✧ Hula ʻAuana (modern hula)


Mālama ʻĀina: This principle of caring for the land is integral, reflecting a deep connection with nature and sustainability.

Aloha Spirit: The concept of aloha encompasses love, peace, and compassion, influencing social interactions and hospitality.

Hawaiian culture is a fascinating tapestry of traditions, stories, and practices that have evolved over centuries. Imagine setting foot on the beautiful islands of Hawaii, surrounded by the vibrant colors of nature, the sweet scent of plumeria, and the soothing sounds of the ocean.

Rooted in Polynesian heritage, Hawaiian culture emphasizes aloha (love and compassion), ʻohana (family), and mālama ʻāina (stewardship of the land), fostering a profound sense of community and respect for nature.

The Hawaiian language(ʻŌlelo Hawaiʻi) and practices such as hula dance and mele (song) serve as vital expressions of identity and history, celebrating the islands' unique stories and spirituality.

Traditional values are embodied in the reverence for gods and goddesses like Pele, the fiery goddess of volcanoes, and Kanaloa, the god of the ocean. Contemporary Hawaiian culture continues to evolve, incorporating influences from diverse cultures while preserving its core traditions and values, creating a dynamic and inclusive society that honors its past while looking toward the future.

Honu

The Honu is a symbol of longevity, safety, and mana (spiritual energy) in Hawaiian culture and their presence brings good luck and peace. The Honu is considered a form of ‘aumakua or ancestral spirit offering lifelong protection, wisdom and guidance. Honu are depicted in ancient Hawaiian petroglyphs, artwork and mythology and represent the eternal link between man, land and sea.
